# FUTURE_DS_03
#  Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis Dashboard

# Project Overview
This project presents a Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI.  
The dashboard evaluates marketing-qualified leads (MQL), closed deals, conversion efficiency, channel performance, and funnel drop-offs.

The objective is to identify performance gaps in the marketing funnel and provide data-driven recommendations to improve lead-to-customer conversion.

# Objectives
- Analyze total marketing-qualified leads (MQL)
- Measure total closed deals
- Calculate overall conversion rate
- Identify funnel drop-offs
- Evaluate marketing channel performance
- Analyze lead generation trends over time
- Provide actionable optimization strategies

# Dashboard Components

# Marketing Funnel Performance
- Funnel visualization showing drop-off from Total Leads to Closed Deals.
- Highlights significant leakage within the conversion pipeline.

# Leads by Marketing Channel
- Displays total leads generated by each channel.
- Organic search and paid search generate the highest lead volume.

# Closed Deals by Channel
- Shows successful deal conversions by channel.
- Identifies high-performing channels in terms of actual revenue contribution.

# Conversion Rate by Marketing Channel
- Compares efficiency of channels.
- Some channels generate fewer leads but show higher conversion rates.

# Lead Generation Trend Over Time
- Visualizes monthly lead generation.
- Identifies seasonal patterns and fluctuations in marketing performance.

# Interactive Filters
- Filter by Origin (Channel)
- Filter by Business Segment
- Filter by Year

Allows dynamic exploration of marketing performance.

# Key Insights

- Significant drop-off exists between total leads and closed deals.
- Organic search generates high lead volume and strong deal conversions.
- Certain channels produce high traffic but lower conversion efficiency.
- Conversion performance varies across business segments.
- Funnel optimization is required to improve overall conversion rate.

# Strategic Recommendations

- Optimize low-converting channels through better targeting.
- Improve lead qualification process before sales engagement.
- Implement automated lead nurturing workflows.
- Allocate budget toward high-converting channels.
- Monitor monthly trends to adjust campaign strategy proactively.
